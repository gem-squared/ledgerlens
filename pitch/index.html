Trust Gate

Two-stage formal audit
before money moves.

⊢ ⊨ ⊬ ⊥
⊢grounded · ⊨inferred · ⊬extrapolated · ⊥unknown
  • L1 P-check — proves the buyer's preconditions hold against evidence (ALLOW / DENY).
